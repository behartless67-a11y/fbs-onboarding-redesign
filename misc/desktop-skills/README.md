# Batten AI Skills — for Claude Desktop

This folder contains five drag-and-drop skills you can load into Claude Desktop to extend Claude with Batten-specific behaviors. No coding required.

---

## What's included

| File | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| `Daily Planner.md` | Turns your calendar + email into a prioritized day plan with prep flags and a Top 3. |
| `Email Helper.md` | Drafts, replies, rewrites, and summarizes email in a clear, professional voice. |
| `Meeting Prep.md` | Builds a one-page prep brief for any meeting — goal, talking points, questions, success. |
| `Executive One-Pager.md` | Turns long docs/threads into a single-page brief with bottom line + recommendation. |
| `Event Run of Show.md` | Generates a minute-by-minute event timeline with prep and day-of checklists. |

---

## How to load a skill into Claude Desktop

1. Open **Claude Desktop**.
2. Go to **Projects** in the left sidebar (or click your profile → Projects).
3. Click **Create Project**. Give it a name like *Daily Planner* or *Batten Helper*.
4. In the project, find the **Project knowledge** section.
5. **Drag the .md file** from this folder into the Project knowledge area. (Or click "Add content" and select the file.)
6. *(Optional but recommended)* In the project's **Custom instructions**, add: *"Apply the skill in the project knowledge to every conversation in this project."*
7. Start a chat **inside that Project** and use the trigger phrases listed below.

---

## How to trigger each skill

Inside the matching Project, type any of these:

### Daily Planner
- "Plan my day."
- "What should I focus on today?"
- "Here's my calendar and inbox — what should I focus on?" *(then paste)*

### Email Helper
- "Help me reply to this:" *(paste thread)*
- "Rewrite this email more professionally:" *(paste draft)*
- "Summarize this thread:" *(paste thread)*
- "Draft an email to [name] about [topic]."

### Meeting Prep
- "Prep me for my [meeting name] meeting."
- "What should I know going into this meeting?" *(paste invite)*

### Executive One-Pager
- "Give me a one-pager."
- "Summarize this for the boss:" *(paste source)*
- "TL;DR this for leadership."

### Event Run of Show
- "Run of show for [event name]."
- "Help me plan [event]."
- "Build me an agenda for [event]."

## Tips for getting good results

- **One Project per skill** is cleanest. You can have all five Projects in your sidebar and click into the one you need.
- **Or one combined Project** if you prefer — drop all five files into a single "Batten Helper" Project and Claude will apply whichever skill matches what you ask. Slightly less precise but simpler.
- **Paste real context.** The more you paste (calendar, email, doc), the better the output. Claude won't see your inbox or calendar unless you paste it in.
- **Anything you paste stays in that conversation** — Claude doesn't send your email or calendar elsewhere. But treat it the same way you'd treat anything you put in a Word doc: don't paste anything you wouldn't email a colleague.

---

## A note on privacy

These skills are designed to work with content you paste into the chat. Nothing is sent anywhere except to Claude (Anthropic's API). Anthropic's standard data policies apply to your Claude Desktop subscription. Do not paste FERPA-protected student records, HR-sensitive personnel info, or anything covered by UVA IRM-003 (Sensitive Data) into Claude unless your account is on a plan that supports it (check with your IT contact).
