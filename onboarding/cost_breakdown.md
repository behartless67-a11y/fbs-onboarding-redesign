## 🎯 ROI Calculation

### Time Saved (Conservative Estimates)

**Current manual work per onboarding:**
- Manager: 2-3 hours tracking down status
- IT: 1-2 hours coordinating via email
- Operations: 1-2 hours checking what's needed
- Admin: 1 hour following up on missing items
- **Total: ~6-9 hours of manual coordination × $50/hour average = $300-450 per onboarding**

**With new system:**
- Automated notifications reduce coordination by 70%
- Clear task tracking eliminates "where are we?" questions
- **Estimated savings: ~$210-315 per onboarding**

**Annual volume:** ~50-60 onboardings per year

**Annual savings:** ~$10,500-18,900 in staff time  
**System cost:** ~$1,200/year  
**Net savings:** ~$9,300-17,700/year

**Payback period:** Immediate (costs less than current inefficiency)
